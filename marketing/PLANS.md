# secret.io plans

let's extend the existing website to add a "pricing" section, under "Download", and remove the "limitations" secton.

Let's use a traditional three-pane view (in desktop) with the following plans.

## free plan

unlimited invites
10 secrets/day
max secret size 10kb
4 hours storage per secret


## pro plan

$10/year
100 secrets/day
max secret size 50kb
24 hours storage per secret


## ops plan

$50/year
1000 secrets/day
max secret size 250kb
unlimited secret storage (defaults to 24 hours)
